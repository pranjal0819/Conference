Proceedings

Proceedings of International Conference on Computational intelligence and communication technology (CICT-2015) is published by IEEE Conference publishing system.

CICT 2017 proceedings will be published by Conference Publishing Services and will be linked with IEEE Xplore. It is mandatory at least one author registers for every paper that is included in the conference proceedings. Proceedings will be made available during the conference. the hard copy of abstracts will also be published with ISBN No.



Special Issues: 

Organizers are successfully negotiated with several International Journals to accommodate special issues on various topics. Expanded versions of CICT 2017 selected papers will be published in Special Issues and Edited Volumes of international referred journals.

Authors of papers presented at CICT 2017 will be invited to submit extended versions of their manuscripts for consideration in the following International Journals if found suitable. 

All accepted & presented papers of the Conference by duly registered authors, will be submitted to IEEE Xplore Digital Library for Publication.
